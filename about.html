Our Mission

MerYunCyber is a digital security initiative born out of necessity—not luxury. We build tools for those who are overlooked by traditional cybersecurity: individuals with no access to advanced tech, activists under threat, people left behind by centralized solutions.

Our goal is simple: make privacy and protection a human right, not a privilege.
